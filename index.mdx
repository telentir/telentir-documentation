## Why Telentir

Telentir combines AI voice agents, campaign automation, SMS follow-up, and live monitoring into one workspace. Use it to:

- Qualify and warm leads 24/7 with natural-sounding AI callers.
- Route live prospects to the best human agent at the perfect moment.
- Track every call, text, and transfer from a single dashboard.
- Keep leadership aligned with real-time performance metrics.
